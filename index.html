About Captain Buttons

Captain Buttons is a Colorado-based pirate character and concertina player who brings lively traditional tunes to public spaces and community events. The act blends maritime flair with the playful irony of being landlocked and mile-high.

Performances are designed to be welcoming and low-impact: short sets, unamplified sound, and a friendly presence that fits pedestrian environments.

What to expect

  • Unamplified acoustic music (concertina)
  • Traditional jigs and polkas, sea tunes, and folk melodies
  • Family-friendly, approachable pirate character
  • Short sets suitable for festivals and strolling crowds
